It focuses on five international standards-setting organisations that are of primary interest to developing countries, namely: International Organisation for Standardisation (ISO), International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC), Codex Alimentarius Commission (Codex), Office International des Epizooties (OIE) and International Plant Protection Convention (IPPC).Download(s):URLAdditional information:Classification:Publications with Development FocusAuthorsKerry Preibisch, Oliver Masakure, Spencer HensonOther bibliographical information:Centre for Food Economics Research, Department of Agricultural and Food Economics, The University of Reading, UK.
