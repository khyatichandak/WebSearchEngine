This study reports on the results of this survey.This is part of a broader NIST Technology Services (TS) effort to assess the impact of standards on globalcompetitiveness and innovation.Download(s):URL (NIST Website)Additional information:Published:2007Other bibliographical information:National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), US Department of Commerce
